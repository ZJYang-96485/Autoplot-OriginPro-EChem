Variable Conversion

Potential Conversion

For stitched sequential files, use step_variable_value. For raw files, use Vf_V_vs_Ref.

Use your reference electrode potential versus SHE.

Formula: E_RHE = E_ref + reference_offset + 0.05916 × pH.

Current Density Conversion

Formula: j_mA_cm2 = Im_A × 1000 / electrode_area_cm2.

For stitched sequence plots, convert step_variable_value to E_RHE and Im_A to j_mA_cm2.

Recommended Final Plot

For the stitched electrochemistry sequence:
  • X: global_time_min
  • Y: j_mA_cm2
  • Step axis: E_RHE vs sequence_index
  • X label: t / min
  • Y label: j / mA cm⁻²
  • Step-axis label: E / V vs. RHE
  • Custom bottom labels: 0.5, 0.2, -0.1, -0.5, -0.9, -0.7, -0.3, 0, 0.4
  • Figure size: 8.2 × 5.3
  • X scale: 0 to 160, major interval 10
  • Y scale: -100 to 5, major interval 20
  • Bottom margin: 0.24
